Welcome to Your Evolutionary Mismatch Insight

In today's fast-paced world, many of us live in ways that sharply contrast with the lifestyle our ancestors evolved to thrive in. The modern environment—filled with processed foods, sedentary routines, and constant digital engagement—can lead to health issues that our bodies and minds aren't fully equipped to handle.

What is the Evolutionary Mismatched Lifestyle Scale (EMLS)?

Researchers have developed the Evolutionary Mismatched Lifestyle Scale (EMLS), a scientifically validated tool designed to measure how your current lifestyle diverges from that of our hunter-gatherer ancestors. This scale examines key areas like diet, physical activity, social connections, and even media use, providing insights into how these mismatches may impact your well-being.

How Does It Work?

You'll answer a series of questions that reflect modern-day behaviors in comparison to ancestral lifestyles. Based on your responses, you'll receive a score showing how closely aligned your lifestyle is with those of our ancestors.

Higher mismatch scores are associated with potential health risks like chronic illness, poor sleep, anxiety, and depression. The goal of this tool is to help you better understand the lifestyle factors that could be affecting your health and how you can make adjustments for a healthier life.
